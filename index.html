The Atmosphere

Dim lights,
warm evenings

Step into Fowlers and leave the world outside. Low lighting, candlelit tables and unhurried service set the scene for an evening entirely your own.

Worcester's finest grill room has welcomed couples, anniversaries and first dates for years — the Western Cape's best-kept romantic secret, tucked away on Church Street.

Whether it's a special occasion or simply a Tuesday worth celebrating, Fowlers is where memories are made over extraordinary food and good company.

Signature Plates

An elegant menu

🥩 Signature Steak

Blue Cheese & Fig Fillet

A prime fillet crowned with creamy blue cheese and sweet fig — rich, refined, and the dish Fowlers is most celebrated for.

🦪 Classic

Carpetbagger Steak

A prime cut stuffed with fresh oysters, grilled to perfection. Indulgent and theatrical — for a night that deserves something extraordinary.

🦑 Seafood

Cajun Calamari

Tender tubes kissed with smoky Cajun spice, grilled to a delicate char. Light, elegant, and completely irresistible.

🍕 Wood-Fired

Thin-crust wood-fired with silky Parma ham, peppery rocket and shaved Parmesan. A beautiful plate to share.
